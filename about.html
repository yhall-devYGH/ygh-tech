Who We Are

Advisory Grounded in Experience,
Delivered with Precision

YGH Tech is a cybersecurity advisory and consulting firm built around a single conviction: security should serve business, not the other way around. We bring experience, independence, and clarity to organizations navigating complex risk environments.

Our Story

Security Advisory That Respects Your Business

YGH Tech was founded on a straightforward observation: too many organizations receive security advice that is technically sound but operationally irrelevant. Recommendations that do not account for team size, budget realities, regulatory context, or strategic direction create a dangerous illusion of progress.

We built YGH Tech to change that. Our advisory engagements are structured around your specific context, not a generic playbook. Every recommendation we deliver is designed to be actionable, proportionate, and aligned to outcomes your leadership team can articulate and defend.

Mission and Approach

What We Believe About Security Advisory

Our approach is shaped by three core convictions that guide every engagement, from the initial conversation to the final deliverable.

Security is a business problem, not just a technical one.

Every vulnerability and every gap has a business consequence. We communicate security risk in terms leadership understands, revenue, liability, reputation, and operational continuity.

Practical beats perfect.

A 95-page report with 200 findings is not useful. We deliver prioritized, actionable output that your team can execute with the context your leadership needs to support it.

Advisory requires trust, and trust requires honesty.

We tell clients what they need to hear. If your security posture has significant gaps, we will say so and give you a credible path to address them.
